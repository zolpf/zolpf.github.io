OUR PROCESS

Mounting

We specialize in framing Photographic prints and we use sophisticated equipment in the process.

Permanent mounting photographic prints using dry mount tissue, 5 mm acid free foam board.

Dry mounting photographic prints is the use of a dry tissue adhesive that is activated by heat ( 85 degrees) from a heat vacuum press.

Dry mounting photographic prints is by far the cleanest and most permanent presentation technique available.

It has been used for many years by all of great masters of photography, and is still widely used.

Matting

Photos should be separated from the glass because the surface of photographs is particularly easy to damage. Any framed photograph should be framed in such a way that the glass does not directly contact the photo. We use single or multiple mats, or special spacers to create an air space between the glass and the print.

Framing

We use picture framing mouldings sourced from Australian and overseas suppliers.

Whenever possible we use and recommend mouldings made of plantation not rainforest timber.

To cut and join picture frame mouldings we use the most advanced equipment.
